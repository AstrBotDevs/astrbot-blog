OSPP is a summer open-source program jointly initiated and long supported by the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Huawei. It encourages students to participate in the development and maintenance of open-source software, helps identify and cultivate outstanding developers, supports healthy open-source communities, and contributes to the open-source software supply chain. For details, please refer to the official OSPP website.

AstrBot is honored to have been selected as one of the open-source software communities for **OSPP 2025**.

- [AstrBot OSPP 2025](https://docs.astrbot.app/ospp/2025.html)
