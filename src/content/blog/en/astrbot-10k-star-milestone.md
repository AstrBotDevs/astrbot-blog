🚀 On **June 24, 2025**, AstrBot officially surpassed **10,000 GitHub Stars** — a milestone that belongs to every developer and user in our community.  
According to GitHub API data, AstrBot has entered the top **3,500** open-source projects globally.  
Through countless commits, iterations, and rounds of feedback, we have witnessed together a milestone built by passion and conviction.

To commemorate this journey, we launched the **10k Stars Commemorative Campaign**, giving limited-edition merchandise to developers and users who have supported AstrBot.

As of **October 11**, all commemorative **AstrBot character standees** have been distributed. A total of **247** people took part in the campaign.

![AstrBot 10k Stars commemorative standee](/source/images/astrbot-10k-star-milestone/cc37b735eb95ceae00eb1263200f66d9_720.png)

🌟 AstrBot's mission remains unchanged:  
to help every user and developer **build their own agents with ease**,  
so that the power of large language models can move from abstraction into real conversations and real-world scenarios.

🛰️ Looking ahead, AstrBot will continue exploring what agents can become.  
We are developing Local Agent software that can run independently on **local computers and mobile devices**,  
with the goal of improving both **productivity workflows and human-like companionship**,  
so agents can become part of everyday life.

> 10k Stars is more than a number.  
> It represents our community, our shared belief, and the starting point of a more intelligent future.

💫 **Next stop? 100k.**  
Let's keep moving forward and light up the next milestone together.
