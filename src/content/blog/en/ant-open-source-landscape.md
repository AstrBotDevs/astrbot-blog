At the 2025 Inclusion Conference on the Bund, themed “Reshaping Innovation and Growth,” Wang Xu, Vice Chair of Ant Group’s Open Source Technical Committee, released version 2.0 of the **Open-Source LLM Development Landscape and Trends**. AstrBot is honored to be featured in the **ChatBot & Knowledge Management** segment under the AI Agent category.

![Source: 527 Ant Technology Day, Wang Xu, Vice Chair of Ant Group’s Open Source Technical Committee](/source/images/ant-open-source-landscape/image.png)

_Source: 527 Ant Technology Day, Wang Xu, Vice Chair of Ant Group’s Open Source Technical Committee. Link: https://antoss-landscape.my.canva.site_

We will continue building a powerful and accessible open-source chatbot framework centered on AI Agents. You are welcome to follow us on [GitHub](https://github.com/AntGroup/AstrBot).
